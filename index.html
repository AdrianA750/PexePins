Who are we

We are a company where pins with original designs of different themes, cartoons, sports, birthday parties, etc. are sold. And we also have an area of ​​non-profit pins which help ecology, endangered animals, and other areas that help to be a better world.

How we Help?

Legally established associations are helped, and propaganda is made monthly to help said associations. The amount collected is given a percentage and the rest will go to producing more pins.
